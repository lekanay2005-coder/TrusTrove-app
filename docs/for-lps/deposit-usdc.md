# Deposit USDC

From the Liquidity Provider Dashboard, click **Deposit USDC**.

### Step 1 — Enter deposit amount

- **Deposit Amount** — the amount of USDC you want to supply to the pool.
- **Estimated Shares** — the number of pool shares you will receive in return. This is calculated using the current share price.

As you enter the amount, the form shows a live preview:

- You deposit: 1,000.00 USDC
- Share price: 1.05 USDC per share
- You receive: 952.38 shares

### Step 2 — Approve token transfer

Before depositing, the smart contract requires permission to transfer USDC from your wallet. Click **Approve USDC** and sign the transaction with Freighter. This sets the allowance.

### Step 3 — Review and sign deposit

Click **Confirm Deposit**. Freighter will prompt you to sign the `deposit` transaction. Once signed, your USDC is transferred to the pool, and your shares are minted on-chain.

### Step 4 — Track your position

Go to the LP Dashboard to track your shares, current USDC value, and yield earned. The value of your shares increases as funded invoices are repaid with discount fees.
